### 1. Hotel S Park

*   **Address/Location:** 1-8-89, 90, 91, Jubilee Pura, Opp. Jubilee Club, Near Sai Ram Theatre, Khammam, Telangana 507003, India.
*   **Google Maps Link:** [View on Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Hotel+S+Park+Khammam)
*   **Google Rating:** 4.1 / 5
*   **Number of Reviews:** ~3,907 reviews
*   **Key Review Highlights:**
    *   *Positives:* Centrally located with easy access to both the railway station and the bus stand. Rooms are widely praised for being spacious, clean, and well-maintained.

*   **Room Types:** Executive Rooms, Superior Rooms, Deluxe Suites.
*   **Approximate Price Range:** ₹2,700 – ₹3,500 per night (plus taxes).
*   **Amenities:** Free Wi-Fi, 24/7 room service, air conditioning, elevator, parking, multi-cuisine restaurant, coffee shop, banquet & conference halls, minibar, tea/coffee maker, CCTV security.
